SpaceSloths

Short Pitch

SpaceSloths is about our passion for space habitation by sending Sloths to Mars.

Long Pitch

The Human race is increasing it's population size at an alarming rate. We aren't seeing the consequences yet, but in future years we may. SpaceSloths is dedicated to coming up with the solution. A solution with an end goal of sending Humans to live on mars. Of course, our lives are too important to put in danger. So, until mars is safe enough for humans, we will use Sloths. Sloths because, well.. what else do they do? SpaceSloths sends sloths to mars and we study how they live on the planet.
